# RECENT-ACHIEVEMENT-ON-UAF
Recent Achievements (2024-25)

At the African U18 & U20 Athletics Championships (July 2025, Nigeria) Uganda secured nine medals across U18/U20 categories. 
Uganda Radionetwork

At the FEASSA Games 2025 (schools level) Uganda’s primary and secondary school athletes had a strong medal haul: e.g., girls’ team won 6 gold, 8 silver, 8 bronze in primary category. 
USSSA

The federation has been undergoing organisational/branding updates: it re-branded from “Uganda Athletics Federation” to “Uganda Athletics” in May 2025, including a new logo, to align with its international body.
